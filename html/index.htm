Welcome to Bugchess

A 2-player turn-based strategy game where simple pieces combine to form powerful units. Outmaneuver your opponent, collect enough pieces, or eliminate them to win.

📖 How to Play (Click to expand)

Your Turn

Each turn, you can do as much or as little as you want:

  • Move — Slide your pieces across the board
  • Place — Add new pieces (you get 2 per turn)
  • Activate — Use special abilities of your pieces

When you're done, press Return or click "End Turn" to let your opponent move.

The Core Mechanic: Merging

When your pieces form specific patterns on the board, they automatically merge into a stronger unit. This is where strategy comes in—plan your placements to create the formations you need.

Winning

You win by either:

  • Eliminating all opponent pieces, or
  • Collecting 20 extra pieces on your side of the board
🎮 Actions (Click to expand)

Moving a Piece

Hover over your piece to see where it can move. Different piece types move different distances and directions. Click the piece to select it, then click the target square.

You can capture enemy pieces by moving onto them—but some stronger pieces can't be captured this way.

Placing a New Piece

You have 2 new basic pieces each turn. Click any empty square to place one. Your available pieces are displayed on the side of the board.

Activating Abilities

Many merged pieces have special powers. Click a piece twice to activate its ability. You'll see where it can target, and just click to confirm.

Undo

Don't worry about making mistakes! Press U or click "Undo" to revert any move during your turn. Learn by experimenting.

🐛 Piece Types (Click to expand)

Tip: Create patterns with your basic pieces to merge them into stronger units.

Basic Piece

Placement & movement

How to Create
Placed directly from your hand (you get 2 per turn)
Movement
One square in any direction (including diagonals)
Special Ability
None—it's just the starting piece
Cross (+)

Creation & shield ability

Pattern
A plus sign: 3 pieces in a vertical line + 3 in a horizontal line, sharing the center
Movement
Slides horizontally and vertically across the board
Special Ability
Shielded — Can't be captured by basic pieces
Bar (— or |)

Creation & gunfire attack

Pattern
Three pieces in a straight line (horizontal or vertical)
Movement
Slides in the direction it's oriented
Special Ability
Gunfire — Shoots bullets in both directions, destroying up to 2 pieces per activation
Queen (◊)

Creation & burst attack

Pattern
A diamond: 8 pieces arranged in a rotated square
Movement
Slides any direction (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal)
Special Ability
Burst — Destroys all adjacent enemy pieces
Sniper (X)

Creation & long-range shot

Pattern
An X: 5 pieces arranged diagonally
Movement
Stationary—it doesn't move
Special Ability
Long Range Shot — Target and destroy any piece on the board (once per turn)
Castle (◽)

Creation & protection effect

Pattern
A small diamond: 4 pieces in a rotated square
Movement
Stationary—it stays in place
Special Ability
Protection — Any adjacent piece of yours is shielded from special abilities
