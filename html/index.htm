Bugchess

A strategy game where simple pieces merge into powerful units — if you place them in the right pattern.

Place pieces in a + pattern → they merge into a Cross

The Core Idea

You start each turn with basic pieces. Place them on the board. When they form a recognizable shape — a line, a plus, a diamond — they automatically merge into a stronger unit with special powers.

Bar

Three in a line. Shoots bullets in both directions.

Sniper

An X pattern. Destroys any piece on the board.

Queen

A large diamond. Moves anywhere, bursts adjacent enemies.

That's where the strategy lives: do you rush three-in-a-row Bars to gun down your opponent's formations, or build toward a Sniper that can pick off any piece on the board?

Quick Start

Place a piece — Click any empty square on the board (you get 2 per turn)

Move a piece — Click one of your pieces, then click where to move it

Use an ability — Double-click a merged piece to activate its power

End your turn — Press Return or click "End Turn"

Undo — Press U to take back any action during your turn

Each turn you can do as much or as little as you want — place, move, attack — then end your turn. Win by eliminating all your opponent's pieces or accumulating 20 extra pieces on your side of the board.

All Pieces

Basic pieces can be placed directly. All others are created by merging basics into a pattern.

Basic Piece — your starting unit

Placed directly from your hand — you get 2 every turn.

Moves: One square in any direction (including diagonals).

Ability: None. It's the building block for everything else.

Bar — three in a line

Pattern: 3 pieces in a straight line (horizontal or vertical).

Moves: Slides along the direction it's oriented.

Ability: Gunfire — Shoots bullets in both directions, destroying up to 2 enemy pieces.

Cross — a plus shape

Pattern: A + sign — 3 vertical + 3 horizontal, sharing the center (5 pieces).

Moves: Slides horizontally and vertically.

Ability: Shielded — Can't be captured by basic pieces.

Castle — a small diamond

Pattern: 4 pieces in a small rotated square (diamond).

Moves: Stationary — it doesn't move.

Ability: Protection — Adjacent friendly pieces are shielded from enemy abilities.

Sniper — an X shape

Pattern: 5 pieces arranged in an X (diagonals).

Moves: Stationary — it doesn't move.

Ability: Long Range Shot — Target and destroy any piece on the board (once per turn).

Queen — a large diamond

Pattern: 8 pieces arranged in a large rotated square (diamond).

Moves: Slides in any direction — horizontal, vertical, or diagonal.

Ability: Burst — Destroys all adjacent enemy pieces.
